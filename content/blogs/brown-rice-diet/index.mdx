## What to expect from here on out

If you’ve been feeling sluggish or hungry too fast, it might be a sign that you need more fiber in your diet. Fiber is essential for maintaining steady energy levels and keeping you full longer. According to the Food and Drug Administration (FDA), adults should aim for 20–25g of fiber per day.

Meeting this goal doesn't have to be complicated. We have curated a list of easy, fiber-rich ingredients you can start adding to your dishes today to improve your overall wellness.
