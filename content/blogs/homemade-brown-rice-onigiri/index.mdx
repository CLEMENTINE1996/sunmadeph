## What to expect from here on out

Making your own Onigiri at home is easier than you think, especially with Sun Made Brown Rice. While traditional Onigiri uses white rice, using brown rice provides a nuttier flavor and a much-needed boost of fiber and antioxidants. 

It’s easy to make, incredibly filling, and perfect for a quick snack or a healthy addition to your lunch box.

### 1. The Perfect Base for Your Snacks
As shown in **feature.jpg**, Sun Made Brown Rice is "The Healthy Switch" you need for your recipes. Because it is 100% whole grain and comes straight from the mill to your hand, it retains the natural stickiness needed to form perfect rice balls while keeping you full for longer.

### 2. Simple Ingredients for a Nutritious Treat
In **recipe.jpg**, we see how simple and fresh your ingredients can be. To get started with your healthy Onigiri, gather these essentials:

* **Sun Made Brown Rice**: Cooked and kept warm for better shaping.
* **Salt & Water**: For seasoning and keeping the rice from sticking to your hands.
* **Nori strips**: For that classic Japanese wrap and added minerals.
* **Your choice of filling**: Tuna mayo, salmon flakes, pickled plum, or fresh veggies.
