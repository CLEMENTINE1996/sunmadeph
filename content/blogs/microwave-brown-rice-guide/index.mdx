## Quick Energy for Your Busiest Days

Don't let cooking time stop you from getting the clean energy and antioxidants you need! We know that life gets hectic, but your nutrition shouldn't have to take a backseat. This easy microwave technique makes our Sun Made Brown Rice accessible even on your busiest days.

By using your microwave, you can enjoy the full benefits of 100% whole grain rice without waiting for a traditional stovetop or rice cooker cycle.

### 1. The Perfect Foundation
The **feature.jpg** image highlights why Sun Made Brown Rice is the ultimate choice for your health. It is "The Healthy Switch" because it is rich in antioxidants and provides a natural source of fiber that keeps you full and energized throughout the day.

--- 

### 2. Step 1: Prep and Soak
As shown in **microwave-1.jpg**, the first step to perfect microwave brown rice is the water-to-rice ratio. For every 1 cup of Sun Made Brown Rice, add 2 cups of water. For the best texture, let it soak for about 30 minutes before you start the cooking process.

### 3. Step 2: The Cooking Process
In **microwave-2.jpg**, we see the active cooking stage. Place your microwave-safe bowl inside and cook on high for 15-20 minutes. It's important to use a container large enough to handle the water as it boils to ensure an even cook without the mess.

<Image
  src="./microwave-2.jpg"
  width="718"

  sizes="100vw"

### 4. Step 3: Steam and Serve
Finally, **microwave-3.jpg** demonstrates the most crucial step: the rest. After the timer goes off, let the rice sit covered inside the microwave for another 5-10 minutes. This allows the steam to finish softening the grains, giving you that perfect, fluffy, wholesome texture every time.

## Microwave Cooking Quick Guide

| Step | Action | Duration |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Preparation** | 1 Cup Rice + 2 Cups Water | 30 Mins Soak |
| **Microwave** | Cook on High Heat | 15–20 Mins |
| **Rest** | Keep Covered (No Heat) | 5–10 Mins |
